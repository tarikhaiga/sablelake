Enhanced offline reality

This is a project that aims to improve help people enhance their offline life and spend less time on their digital devices. We recognize the benefits of digital technology and its capabilities, that is why we are not seeking an alternative but a complement.
Everyone should be information conscious and maintain a healthy information diet. As a result, we are designing "offline products", writing articles, discussing research and compiling a repository of information on how to enhance your offline life.
